This Privacy Policy explains how Britari Properties Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal information when you use the MyBritari mobile application. By using MyBritari, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1 Who We Are

Britari Properties Ltd is a Nigerian real estate company that enables individuals to purchase and pay for land plots across premium estates in Nigeria through an installment-based system.

MyBritari is our official mobile application, available on Android and iOS, through which clients reserve plots, manage contracts, make payments, and access their documents.

For all privacy matters, contact us at: support@britariproperties.com

3 How We Use Your Data

  • Account management — creating and maintaining your account, authenticating your identity
  • KYC verification — verifying your identity to comply with Nigerian financial regulations before issuing property allocation letters
  • Contract management — creating, maintaining, and displaying your property contracts, payment schedules, and documents
  • Payment processing — recording payments, reconciling balances, and issuing receipts
  • Communication — sending push notifications, email, and in-app notifications about your account, payments, and promotions
  • Customer support — responding to your enquiries and resolving disputes
  • Referral programme — tracking referral activity and processing referral bonuses
  • Loan processing — reviewing and administering loan applications
  • Legal compliance — complying with applicable Nigerian laws and regulations
  • Security — detecting and preventing fraud, unauthorised access, and abuse

4 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal grounds:

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to fulfil your property purchase contract
  • Legal obligation — KYC data collected to comply with Nigerian anti-money laundering and real estate regulations
  • Legitimate interests — fraud prevention, security, and improving our services
  • Consent — marketing communications and optional features (you may withdraw consent at any time)

6 Data Retention

  • Account & contract data — retained for the duration of your account plus 7 years after your last transaction, as required by Nigerian financial record-keeping regulations
  • KYC documents — retained for a minimum of 5 years following the completion of your contract, as required by law
  • Payment records — retained for 7 years for accounting and legal compliance
  • Support messages — retained for 2 years
  • Push tokens — deleted when you log out or uninstall the app
  • Deleted accounts — personal data anonymised within 90 days of account deletion, except where retention is required by law

7 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time via the app or by emailing us

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@britariproperties.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Account deletion: You may request deletion of your account by contacting our support team. Note that active contracts and outstanding payment obligations remain legally binding regardless of account deletion.

8 Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your personal data:

  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS / TLS
  • Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes — never in plain text
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens with expiry and secure storage on your device
  • Biometric authentication is processed entirely on your device — we never receive your fingerprint or face data
  • Payment card data is handled exclusively by Paystack (PCI-DSS compliant) — we never store card numbers
  • Database access is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • API endpoints are rate-limited to prevent brute-force attacks

Despite these measures, no internet transmission is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

9 Children's Privacy

MyBritari is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has registered an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
